**TalkingToad**

*Free, plain-English SEO audits built for small businesses and
nonprofits*

TalkingToad finds the technical problems that keep your website from
showing up in Google and AI search — then explains each one in plain
language and, for WordPress sites, lets you apply fixes from the app. It
runs in your browser, costs nothing, and needs no installation or
technical skill.

**What it does**

**▸ Crawls your whole site** and checks every page (500 by default,
adjustable) for broken links, missing or duplicate titles and
descriptions, redirect problems, heading errors, and security gaps.

**▸ Scores your site 0–100** and ranks the top fixes by impact and
effort, so you know exactly where to start.

**▸ Lets an admin apply WordPress fixes from the app** — it uses AI to
suggest content like titles, descriptions and tags where appropriate;
you review and approve each one before it's applied (Yoast and Rank Math
supported). No code.

**▸ Checks AI readiness** — how easily tools like ChatGPT and Google's
AI can read and quote your content, plus llms.txt support.

**▸ Produces client-ready reports** — branded PDF audits and CSV exports
you can hand to a board, funder, or developer.
